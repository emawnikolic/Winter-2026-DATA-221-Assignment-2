the 10 most frequently used words with how many times they appear.

a text file, and shows the most common 5.

the results while calculating the average grade.

their attendance and internet access.

Question 6:

unemployment rates between groups.

Question 7:

title and first main paragraph.

Question 8:

and saves them to a text file.

Question 9:

the structured data to a CSV file.
